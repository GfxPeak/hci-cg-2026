Summary of the "Who Paid" Problem

This interaction highlights how the Gulf of Evaluation can occur if an app is poorly designed. While GOMS models the expert speed of finding the payer, Norman's model explains why a user might struggle if the interface does not clearly signal who paid versus who owes[cite: 28, 29].
